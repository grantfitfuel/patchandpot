Support if you need it now

If you or someone else is in immediate danger call 999 (UK or Ireland) or 112 (EU-wide). For urgent medical advice that is not life-threatening, use your nation’s NHS or HSE service below.

UK-wide services

  • Samaritans — free 24/7 listening support
  • Shout — text support, free and confidential
  • Mind — mental health information and helplines
  • Support After Suicide Partnership
  • SOBS — Survivors of Bereavement by Suicide
  • National Domestic Abuse Helpline (Refuge)
  • Men’s Advice Line
  • Carers UK
  • Cruse Bereavement Support

England

  • NHS 111 — urgent medical help
  • NHS — mental health support
  • Victim Support England and Wales
  • Carers UK — support in England
  • Cruse — bereavement support in England

Scotland

  • NHS 24
  • Breathing Space
  • Domestic Abuse and Forced Marriage Helpline
  • Carers Scotland
  • Cruse Scotland

Wales

  • NHS 111 Wales
  • CALL Mental Health Helpline for Wales
  • Live Fear Free — domestic abuse support
  • Victim Support Wales
  • Carers Wales
  • Cruse Cymru

Northern Ireland

  • NHS 111 Northern Ireland
  • Minding Your Head
  • 24-hour Domestic & Sexual Abuse Helpline
  • Victim Support NI
  • Carers Northern Ireland
  • Cruse Northern Ireland

Ireland (Éire)

  • HSE — Health Service Executive
  • HSE — Mental Health Services
  • Samaritans Ireland — 116 123
  • Pieta — crisis support & suicide prevention
  • Women’s Aid Ireland — domestic abuse support
  • Safe Ireland — domestic violence services
  • Victim Support Ireland
  • Family Carers Ireland
  • Irish Hospice Foundation — bereavement support
