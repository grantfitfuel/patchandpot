Recipes

Budget-friendly dishes using balcony & windowsill harvests. Clear allergen labels. Simple swaps.

Quick Salads

Golden Beet, Orange & Basil Salad

10 mins · serves 2

VGFDF
  • 2 small golden beetroots, very thinly sliced (raw or steamed & cooled)
  • 1 orange, juiced (plus a few segments, optional)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 8–10 basil leaves, torn
  • Pinch ground cinnamon
  • Sea salt & black pepper
  1. Toss beet slices with orange juice, olive oil, cinnamon, salt & pepper.
  2. Fold in basil and any orange segments. Taste, adjust acid/salt. Serve cold.

Swap: use lemon if no orange; mint instead of basil. Add toasted seeds for crunch.

Tomato, Basil & Bread “Pantry Panzanella”

12 mins · serves 2

VDF (if no cheese)
  • 2 cups ripe tomatoes, chunked
  • 2 cups stale bread cubes
  • 2 tbsp red wine vinegar · 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 garlic clove, grated · basil handful
  • Salt/pepper · optional: torn mozzarella
  1. Toast bread cubes in a dry pan 2–3 mins.
  2. Toss tomatoes with vinegar, oil, garlic, salt, pepper; add bread & basil. Rest 5 mins and serve.

Kale Ribbon Salad with Chilli Oil

8 mins · serves 2

VGFDF
  • 3 cups young kale, finely sliced
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice · 2 tsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp chilli oil (or flakes + extra oil)
  • Pinch salt · 1 tsp sesame seeds
  1. Massage kale with lemon, oil & salt for 30–60s.
  2. Toss through chilli oil and sesame. Serve with eggs or toast.

Simple Mains

Cherry Tomato & Garlic Oil Pasta

20 mins · serves 2

VDFcan be GF
  • 180–200 g pasta (GF if needed)
  • 2–3 tbsp olive oil · 3 garlic cloves, sliced
  • 2 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Pinch chilli flakes · basil or parsley
  • Salt/pepper · optional: grated cheese
  1. Cook pasta in salted water.
  2. Meanwhile gently sizzle garlic in oil, add tomatoes & chilli, cook 5–6 mins until saucy.
  3. Toss with pasta & herbs. Season and serve.

Herby Eggs on Toast

10 mins · serves 1–2

V
  • 2–3 eggs
  • 1 tbsp butter or oil
  • Chives/parsley/basil, finely chopped
  • 2 slices toast (GF if needed)
  • Salt/pepper
  1. Scramble or fry eggs in butter/oil; fold in herbs.
  2. Season and pile onto toast. Add tomato slices if you’ve got them.

Chilli & Basil Fried Rice

15 mins · serves 2

VDFcan be GF
  • 2 cups cooked cold rice
  • 1 onion, sliced · 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 chilli (or flakes) · handful basil
  • 2 tbsp oil · 1–2 tbsp soy/tamari
  • Optional: peas or chopped greens
  1. Stir-fry onion/garlic in oil, add chilli & rice; fry until hot & a little crispy.
  2. Stir through soy/tamari and basil. Fold in peas/greens at the end.

Soups & Sides

Tomato & Basil Soup

25 mins · serves 3–4

VGF
  • 1 onion & 2 garlic cloves, chopped
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 large tin tomatoes (or 4 cups fresh)
  • 1 cup water/stock · basil
  • Salt/pepper · optional swirl of yoghurt/cream
  1. Soften onion/garlic in oil, add tomatoes & water; simmer 12–15 mins.
  2. Blend smooth, add basil, season and serve.

Minted Peas

7 mins · serves 2–3

VGF
  • 2 cups peas (frozen fine)
  • 1 tbsp butter or olive oil
  • Mint leaves, chopped · squeeze of lemon
  • Salt/pepper
  1. Warm peas in a pan with butter/oil until hot.
  2. Stir in mint & lemon; season and serve.

Chive Yoghurt

2 mins · makes ~1 cup

VGF
  • 200 g plain yoghurt
  • Small handful chives, finely snipped
  • Pinch salt, pepper & lemon zest
  1. Mix all ingredients. Use as dip, dollop for soups, or spread for wraps.

Hydration Helpers

Simple sips that use your herbs.

  • Cucumber–Mint Water: slices + mint sprigs in cold water.
  • Lemon–Basil Cooler: lemon slices + torn basil + ice.
  • Strawberry–Thyme: bruised berries + thyme in sparkling water.

Tip: add a pinch of salt after hot days or workouts.

Allergen Key

V: vegetarian · GF: gluten-free · DF: dairy-free · “can be” = easy swaps.

Waste-Less Ideas

Freeze herb stems in oil cubes; bake stale bread for crumbs; simmer tomato skins in stock.
