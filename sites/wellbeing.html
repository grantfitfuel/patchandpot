2-Minute Calm

Three micro-exercises to steady breathing and attention.

  1. Square breath (4-4-4-4) × 4 rounds.
  2. Grounding: 5 see · 4 feel · 3 hear · 2 smell · 1 taste.
  3. Soft gaze: follow one leaf for 60 seconds, then exhale slowly.
