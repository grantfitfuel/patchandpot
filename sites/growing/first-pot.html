Your First Pot

A single pot, some peat-free compost, and a handful of seeds is all you need.

Setup

  1. Pot with drainage holes (20–30 cm wide for salads/herbs).
  2. Line base with mesh/paper to stop compost escaping.
  3. Fill with peat-free compost to 2 cm below rim; mix slow-release feed for fruiting crops.

Plant

  1. Sow thinly (lettuce, basil, parsley) or plant seedlings.
  2. Water until a little drains from the bottom.
  3. Light — aim for 6+ hours sun; rotate weekly.

Care

  • Water when top inch is dry (deeper rather than little splashes).
  • Harvest outer leaves first; never strip plants bare.
  • Top-dress with compost mid-season if growth slows.
