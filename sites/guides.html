Growing Guides

Step-by-step guides to help you start, plan and enjoy mindful growing.

Your First Pot

Starting simple means you actually get growing. One pot is all you need.

  • Choose your pot: at least 20–25cm deep, with drainage holes.
  • Drainage: add broken crockery, grit or even polystyrene at the base.
  • Soil: multi-purpose compost mixed with a little grit for airflow.
  • Seeds: start with lettuce, radish, or basil — quick wins.
  • Watering: keep soil moist, not soggy. Stick a finger in to test.

What to Sow & When

A UK-friendly sowing calendar for balcony and pot growers:

  • Spring (Mar–May): lettuce, spinach, beetroot, herbs.
  • Summer (Jun–Aug): tomatoes, peppers, dwarf beans.
  • Autumn (Sep–Oct): kale, pak choi, winter salads.
  • Winter (Nov–Feb): hardy greens, garlic, broad beans under cover.

2-Minute Calm

Mindful pauses while tending your plants:

  1. Pause. Place both hands gently on the pot.
  2. Inhale slowly for a count of 4, feel the scent of soil or herbs.
  3. Exhale for a count of 6, soften your shoulders.
  4. Notice one small detail on the plant you hadn’t before.

Repeat once, then return to your day — calmer, lighter.
