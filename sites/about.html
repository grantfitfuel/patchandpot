About Patch & Pot

Patch & Pot is about making gardening accessible and mindful. Whether you’ve got a single pot on a balcony or a patch in the garden, you can grow food, flowers, and calm.

We emphasise affordable, practical, and inclusive gardening — with tips for growing from seed, recycling containers, and creating sensory planting spaces.
