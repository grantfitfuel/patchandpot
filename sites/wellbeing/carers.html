Carer Support

Fast resets, boundaries, and tiny habits to protect your energy.

3 quick resets

  • Box-breathing 3× (inhale 4, hold 4, exhale 4, hold 4).
  • Look far away for 20 seconds to rest visual load.
  • Step outside; notice 3 sounds, 3 sights, 3 sensations.

Boundaries that stick

Use the “Yes, if…” script to keep empathy and limits:

“Yes, I can help, if we do X first / for 20 minutes / after lunch.”
