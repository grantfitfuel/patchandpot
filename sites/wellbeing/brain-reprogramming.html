Tiny repeated cues

Re-programming Your Brain

Habits stick when tied to cues. Use plant care as your daily anchor.

Attach to Existing Routines

  • After making tea → check moisture of one pot.
  • After brushing teeth → mist herbs if top is dry.
  • After dinner → pick 3 leaves for tomorrow’s meal.

Make Wins Visible

  • Keep a tiny “harvest jar” of twist-ties or clipped stems.
  • Take a weekly photo of the same pot.
  • Tell someone what grew — small social reward.

Fail-Safe Mode

  • Self-watering planters and wicking cords.
  • Slow-release feed to smooth out attention dips.
  • Group pots by thirst; water the “thirsty corner”.
