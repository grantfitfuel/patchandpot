Language that helps

Neurolinguistics

Tiny language swaps that reduce threat and make action easier.

Swap the Frame

  • From “I must” → “I could try one small thing”.
  • From “I failed” → “I learned X; next time I’ll try Y”.
  • From “I never stick at it” → “I’m experimenting with repeats”.

Say It Out Loud

  • “Watering one pot counts.”
  • “I can do 60 seconds.”
  • “This is allowed to be tiny.”

Environmental Prompts

  • Place a note on the kettle: “Sip, then check soil.”
  • Put scissors next to herbs: “Snip 3 leaves.”
  • Keep the watering can visible.
