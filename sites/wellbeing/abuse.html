You deserve safety

Abuse

If it isn’t safe, it isn’t love. Here are quiet steps to increase safety and get help.

Safety plan Where to get help

Breadcrumb: Home › Wellbeing › Abuse

Signals & code words

Agree a simple phrase with a trusted person that means “call me now” or “send help”.

Add a trusted contact

Pack a small bag

ID, meds, cash, charger, spare keys. Hide safely or leave with someone you trust.

Find local services

Digital safety

Private browsing, new email, log out shared devices. Consider a cheap separate phone.

If danger is immediate

UK & ROI

National lines (Women’s Aid, Men’s Advice Line, LGBT+ support). Call emergency if at risk.

Contacts

Document incidents

Dates, screenshots, photos if safe. Keep somewhere private.

Stabilise thoughts

After leaving

Safe accommodation, money, legal advice. Healing takes time — support helps.
