Your First Pot

What you need

  • 1 pot with drainage holes (20–30cm)
  • Multi-purpose compost
  • Seeds or starter plant (herbs or salad leaves are great)
  • Watering can or jug

Quick steps

  1. Fill pot with compost, leaving 2–3cm at the top.
  2. Sow seeds lightly (or plant the starter), cover gently.
  3. Water until evenly moist, not soggy.
  4. Place where it gets light; check daily.
