Support if you need it now

If you or someone else is in immediate danger call 999 (UK/Ireland) or 112 (EU). For urgent medical advice that is not life-threatening, use your nation’s NHS or HSE service below.

UK-wide services
  • Samaritans — 24/7 listening support
  • Shout — text support, free & confidential
  • Mind — mental health information
  • Support After Suicide Partnership
  • SOBS — Survivors of Bereavement by Suicide
  • National Domestic Abuse Helpline (Refuge)
  • Men’s Advice Line
  • Carers UK
  • Cruse Bereavement Support
England

  • NHS — mental health support
