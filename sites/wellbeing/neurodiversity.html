ADHD / Autistic-friendly

Neurodiversity

Plant care routines that work with your brain, not against it.

Design for Low Friction

  • Keep a small watering can next to pots (reduce steps).
  • Use self-watering planters to stretch intervals.
  • Schedule a single “plant check” anchor (e.g., after brushing teeth).

Sensory Wins

  • Soft herbs (mint/basil) for smell, textures that calm.
  • Noise: add a small water fountain or rustle of grasses.
  • Lighting: morning light check-in by a bright window.

Time Blindness Helpers

  • Use a visual timer or set “water Tue/Fri” reminders.
  • Micro-tasks: 60-second checks beat 20-minute chores.
  • Keep a visible “to water” tag on dry pots.
