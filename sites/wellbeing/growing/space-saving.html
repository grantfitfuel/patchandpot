Small spaces

Space-Saving Ideas

Go vertical, stack tiers, and choose compact varieties for balconies and sills.

Vertical & Tiered

  • Use shelves, wall planters, stackable pots.
  • Keep thirsty plants lower; sun-lovers higher.
  • Secure everything against wind.

Compact Crops

  • Bush tomatoes, dwarf chillies, baby leaf mixes.
  • Chives, parsley, thyme, basil.
  • Radishes, cut-and-come-again salads.

Smart Watering

  • Self-watering planters or trays with capillary matting.
  • Group pots by thirst to water efficiently.
  • Mulch with bark/strips of cardboard to slow evaporation.
