Shift the story

Thought Reframing

When a thought blocks you, shrink the task and step around it.

Reframe Library

  • “It’s too much.” → “What is 1% of it?”
  • “I’m behind.” → “I’ll do one tiny thing now.”
  • “I’ll ruin it.” → “Plants are forgiving; I can learn.”

One-Pot Plan

Pick one pot, one task, 2 minutes. Done is the win.

  1. Touch soil → if dry, water a little.
  2. Snip one herb leaf.
  3. Rotate the pot 90° for even light.

When Stuck

  • Change location: balcony/window for 60 seconds.
  • Change posture: stand, breathe out longer.
  • Change scale: pick the smallest next step.
