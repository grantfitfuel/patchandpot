Quick Salads

Golden Beet, Orange & Basil Salad

20 mins + cooling · serves 2 · ~220 kcal | P 4 g · C 14 g · F 16 g

V GF DF

  • 2 small golden beetroots
  • 1 orange (juice + optional zest)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 8–10 basil leaves, torn
  • Sea salt & black pepper
  • Pinch ground cinnamon
  1. Cook the beets until tender, cool and peel.
  2. Slice and toss with orange & oil dressing.
  3. Add basil and a light dusting of cinnamon.

Simple Mains

Courgette & Herb Fritters

20 mins · serves 2 · ~320 kcal | P 11 g · C 18 g · F 22 g

V DF can be GF

  • 2 small courgettes, grated & salted
  • 1 egg · 3 tbsp flour
  • Chopped herbs · salt · pepper
  • Oil for frying
  1. Squeeze courgettes very dry.
  2. Mix with egg, flour, herbs, seasoning.
  3. Pan-fry spoonfuls until golden both sides.

Beetroot & Lentil Bake

35 mins · serves 3–4 · ~280 kcal | P 12 g · C 32 g · F 8 g

V GF DF

  • 2–3 beetroots, diced
  • 1 onion · 2 cups cooked lentils
  • Garlic · herbs · 1 cup stock or passata
  1. Sauté onion & garlic.
  2. Add beetroot, lentils, liquid; season.
  3. Bake covered 25–30 mins until tender.

V DF GF
