Stay ahead

Pests & Problems

Spot issues early, act gently, and keep your small space productive.

Aphids / Whitefly

  • Blast with water; repeat. Wipe undersides of leaves.
  • Use dilute soapy spray (a drop of mild soap in water).
  • Add companion flowers and encourage ladybirds.

Over/Under Watering

  • Over: yellowing, soft leaves — let soil dry a little, improve drainage.
  • Under: wilting, dry soil — water deeply; mulch to slow evaporation.
  • Lift pots on feet so they don’t sit in water.

Feeding & Light

  • Slow-release pellets for tomatoes/chillies; liquids fortnightly in season.
  • 6+ hours sun; rotate pots weekly for even growth.
  • Thin crowded seedlings to improve airflow.
