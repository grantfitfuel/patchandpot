Troubleshooting

Diagnosing Plant Problems

Start simple: water, light, airflow. Then feed and pest checks. Most issues live there.

Quick Triage

  1. Water: check top 2–3 cm and pot weight.
  2. Light: enough hours? Rotate weekly.
  3. Airflow: thin congested bits; space pots.
  4. Feed: last feed date and strength?
  5. Pests: underside leaf check with a torch.
