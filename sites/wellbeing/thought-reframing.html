Thought Reframing

Catch → Check → Change. Not positive-washing — just a kinder, more accurate take.

3 steps Examples

Breadcrumb: Home › Wellbeing › Thought Reframing

1) Catch

Notice the thought. Write it. Say “I’m having the thought that…”.

See examples

2) Check

Evidence for/against? What would I tell a friend?

Helpful wording

3) Change

Choose a truer, kinder thought. Pair it with one small action.

Micro-habit it

“I always fail.”

→ “Lately it’s been tough — and I’m still trying.”

Try an easy win

“It must be perfect.”

→ “Enough to grow a leaf.”

Start small

“I can’t cope.”

→ “I can breathe for 30 seconds.”
