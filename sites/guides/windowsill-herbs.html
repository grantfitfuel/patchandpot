Indoor wins

Windowsill Herb Garden

Fresh sprigs on demand. Start with 3–5 herbs and learn their light & water personalities.

Best Herbs for Indoors

HerbLightWaterNotes
BasilBright, warmEven moisturePinch tips weekly; hates cold draughts.
ParsleyBright to part shadeMoist, not wetCut outer stems first.
ChivesBrightModerateShear and it re-sprouts quickly.
CorianderBright, coolEven moisturePrefers cooler rooms; sow little-and-often.
ThymeVery brightDryerGritty mix; don’t overwater.
Containers: 9–12 cm pots with saucers or a narrow trough with drainage holes.

Routine

  • Turn pots weekly for even light.
  • Water when the top 2 cm is dry; thyme/rosemary like longer gaps.
  • Feed lightly every 2 weeks once growing well.
  • Harvest little and often — never strip a plant bare.

Common Issues

  • Leggy basil: not enough light — move closer to the window or add a small LED bar.
  • Yellow parsley: waterlogging — use a freer-draining mix and empty saucers.
  • Coriander bolts: too warm — grow in a cooler spot and succession-sow.
