Quick Salads

Golden Beet, Orange & Basil Salad

20 mins + cooling · serves 2 · ~220 kcal | P 4 g · C 14 g · F 16 g

VGFDF
  • 2 small golden beetroots
  • 1 orange (juice + optional zest)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 8–10 basil leaves, torn
  • Sea salt & black pepper
  • Pinch ground cinnamon (for sprinkling)
  1. Cook the beets: wrap in foil and roast at 190 °C for 35–50 mins or steam 25–35 mins until tender.
  2. Cool slightly, peel and slice thinly.
  3. Whisk orange juice (and zest) with olive oil, salt & pepper. Toss gently with the sliced beets. Chill if preferred.
  4. Plate up, add basil, then sprinkle cinnamon lightly over the top before serving.

  2. Rest 5 mins to soak up flavour.
