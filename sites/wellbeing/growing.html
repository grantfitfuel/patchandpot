Minimum Kit

Pot with drainage, peat-free compost, seeds/seedlings, watering can or jug. Optional: slow-release feed.

Sun & Water

Aim for 6+ hours sun. Water deeply when the top inch dries; don’t leave pots sitting in trays of water.

Quick Wins

Cut-and-come-again lettuce, mixed herbs, dwarf tomatoes, chillies. One pot is enough to begin.
