Perennials & shrubs for cutting

Bulbs, corms & tubers

  • Spring: tulips (plant Nov for long stems), narcissus, alliums.
  • Summer: gladioli (stagger plantings), lilies (pots; stake), dahlias (start in pots under cover).
  • Autumn: colchicum for interest (not for vases).

Dahlias love rich soil and consistent moisture; lift/store tubers in cold, wet northern gardens.

Harvest & conditioning

  • Cut early morning; clean buckets; strip lower foliage.
  • Stage of cut: bud colour-showing (snapdragon), first petals open (cosmos), half open (zinnia).
  • Immediate re-cut under water; use floral preservative (or sugar + citric + a drop of bleach).
