Your First Pot

A simple, forgiving setup for salads, herbs, and easy veg — windowsill, doorstep, or balcony.

Step-by-step

  1. Prep the pot (drainage hole cover).
  2. Fill with compost to 2 cm below rim.
  3. Plant seedling or sow thinly.
  4. Water slowly until just draining.
  5. Place in best light, rotate weekly.
