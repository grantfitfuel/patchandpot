Keep it tiny

30-second habit > 30-minute goal. Water one pot. One breath. One stretch.

Supportive language

Track lightly

Tick a box, move a bead on a string. Visible progress fuels habit loops.

Reward

Ideas to try

Reframing

Environmental cues

Plant by the kettle, gloves by the door, watering can by the sink.
