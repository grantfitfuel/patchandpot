Plant friends

Companion Planting

Pair crops that support each other — flavour, pollinators, and pest control.

Great Combos

  • Tomato + Basil: flavour friends; similar water/light needs.
  • Chilli + Marigold: flowers attract pollinators, distract pests.
  • Kale + Chives: chives help deter aphids; both suit pots.

Things to Avoid

  • Don’t overcrowd — roots and leaves need airflow.
  • Don’t mix very thirsty with drought-tolerant plants in one pot.
  • Watch heights — taller plants shouldn’t shade short sun-lovers.

Pollinator Boost

  • Add edible flowers (nasturtiums, violas).
  • Let a few herbs flower (basil, chives) for bees.
  • Keep flowers pesticide-free.
