Foundations

Compost, Soil & Mulch

Good mix = fewer problems. Aim for structure, drainage, and steady moisture.

Reliable Container Mix (Baseline)

  • 60% peat-free multi-purpose compost
  • 30% fine bark or wood fibre
  • 10% perlite or grit
  • Slow-release fertiliser (10–12 weeks)
Heavy feeders: add 5–10% worm castings or 2–4% biochar pre-charged with liquid feed.

Drainage vs Retention

If pots stay soggy

  • Increase perlite/grit to 15–20%.
  • Add more bark; use pot feet.

If pots dry in a day

  • Blend in 10% coir or fine bark.
  • Mulch surface 2–3 cm.

Mulch Options

MulchBest forNotes
Fine barkMost containersClean look; reduces splash and weeds.
StrawTomatoes, courgettesKeeps fruit clean; check for slugs.
Coco chipsDry balconiesLong-lasting; light weight.
GritLavender, rosemaryImproves crown dryness for med herbs.

Refreshing Tired Compost

  • Remove roots; mix in one-third fresh compost and a handful of slow-release feed.
  • Add 5% worm castings for biology.
  • For disease issues (blight/mildew), replace more and clean pots/stakes.
