Safety first

Abuse & Safeguarding

You are not to blame. You deserve safety and support.

Signs

  • Control over money, phone, movements.
  • Isolation from friends/family.
  • Fear of partner’s reactions.

Steps

  • Tell one safe person. Agree code words.
  • Keep essentials ready. Record incidents if safe.
  • Contact local services / police if in danger.
