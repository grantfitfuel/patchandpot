Reprogramming Your Brain

Brains change with what we repeat. Use tiny loops that are easy on tired days and still create momentum.

Cue → Tiny Action → Reward

  • Cue: kettle on
  • Tiny action: 3 squats or water a plant
  • Reward: sip + tick a box

Habit Ladder

Define a “bare minimum” version so you never drop to zero.

  • Water one plant → water two → tidy one pot.

Streaks That Forgive

Allow 2 skips per week. Restart without drama. The system is the win.

10-Min Focus Box

  • Silence pings; one tab; timer 10
  • Do one small chunk; stop while it’s easy

State Shift

  • Breath: 4-4-4-4 × 4 cycles
  • Light: step to a window
  • Move: 60-second stretch

3-Line Gratitude

One sentence each: something you saw, someone you appreciate, something you did.
